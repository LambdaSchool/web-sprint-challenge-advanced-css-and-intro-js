1. How would you describe acessibility on the web to someone new to programming?

Accesibility on the web refers to easy-access tools to allow people who are impaired in the following 
navigate your page easily:

1) impaired sight
2) impaired hearing

(People with hearing or sight impairments are likely to use different devices that further allow them to see or hear content on the page easily)



2. Talk about 3 different things you can do to ensure your website is accessible. 

1) Screen Readers
2) Adding alternative ways to read / hear content
3) Use breakpoints to allow your content to fit different devices so they may be able to see the content easily
